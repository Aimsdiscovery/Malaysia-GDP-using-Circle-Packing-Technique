Malaysia's GDP Data Visualization

Welcome to the visualization of Malaysia's GDP data using the circle packing technique. This method represents hierarchical data with nested circles, where each circle corresponds to a data point, sized according to its value. Larger circles contain smaller ones, clearly depicting data relationships and making it easier to understand complex datasets in an intuitive and engaging manner.
